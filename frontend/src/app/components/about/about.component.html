Filfabs - Filter your Fabrics

The textile material database Filfabs is a production by the HTW Berlin - University of Applied Sciences. It was developed by students and lecturers from the faculty 4 "School of Computing, Communication & Business" and the faculty 5 "School of Design & Cultures" during a semester project. The structure of the database, the fabric data sets, the pictures and the corporate design was developed by faculty 5 and the implementation was made by faculty 4.

The database is meant for 3D-product developer, fashion designer, Fashion product developer and people who wish to be inspired. All textile materials are tested according to the ISO-Standards within the laboratory of the HTW-Berlin and are subject to the highest standards. If you wish to receive further information, don't hesitate to reach out to us by using the contact form under "FAQ".

We hope you enjoy exploring FILFABS.
