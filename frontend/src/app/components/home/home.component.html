Filfabs - Filter your Fabrics

Filfabs is a textile material database for various fields of applications.

As a 3D product developer you can find textile materials based on your needs and receive ready to use data for all common 3D-Programs.

As a Fashion Designer you can find inspiration on what's new on the textile market.

As a Fashion product developer you can receive extensive data sets for textile materials, tested according to ISO Standards.
